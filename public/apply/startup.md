## Early stage shouldn't mean limited tools

We know what it's like to build with limited resources. That's why we're giving early-stage companies **50% off Pullse for 12 months**—so you can focus on building your product, not managing support infrastructure.

## Program Benefits

### 50% Discount
- **Standard Plan**: $24.50/seat/month (normally $49)
- **Pro Plan**: $39.50/seat/month (normally $79)
- **12-month commitment** required
- Credits still at standard rates ($0.08-0.10 each)

### What's Included
- **15 seats included** (expand beyond 15 at discounted rate)
- **All Pro features** available (Auto-QA, unlimited help centers, unlimited copilot profiles)

- No automatic price increases during your first year
- Cancel anytime (no penalties after 12-month period)

## Eligibility Requirements

Must meet **all** of the following:

### Company Age
- Founded **within the last 3 years** (since 2022 or later)
- Active business with product in market or launching soon
- Not a current Pullse customer

### Revenue/Funding
Meet **one** of the following:
- Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) **<$2M**
- Total funding raised **≤$5M**

### Use Case
- Planning to use Pullse as primary customer support platform
- At least 3 team members who will use the platform

## FAQs

**Q: Can I upgrade from Standard to Pro during the 12 months?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade anytime and keep the 50% discount on Pro pricing.

**Q: What happens after 12 months?**
A: You convert to standard pricing. We'll notify you 60 days before the transition with options to adjust your plan.

**Q: Can I add more seats during the discount period?**
A: Yes, additional seats during the first 12 months also receive the 50% discount.

**Q: Do I have to pay upfront?**
A: No, standard monthly billing applies. You just pay 50% of the regular monthly rate.

**Q: Can we cancel anytime?**
A: You must commit to 12 months. After that, cancel anytime with 30-day notice.

**Q: Does the discount apply to credit usage?**
A: No, credits are charged at standard rates ($0.08 pre-committed or $0.10 PAYG). The 50% discount only applies to per-seat pricing.

**Q: Can I apply if I'm already using Pullse on a trial?**
A: Yes, as long as you haven't started a paid subscription yet. Apply during your trial and we'll convert you to startup pricing.

**Q: What if we raise funding during the 12 months and exceed the eligibility limits?**
A: You keep the discount for the full 12 months. Eligibility is only checked at application time.

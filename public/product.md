# Pullse Platform Overview

## Your entire support stack. One platform. One price.

Pullse replaces Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk, plus 10+ fragmented tools with a single AI-native platform built for modern support teams.

## Core Capabilities

### Unified Inbox
- **Email Integration**: Connect unlimited email addresses, shared inboxes with automatic threading
- **Live Chat**: Embeddable widget with customizable branding and proactive messaging
- **Voice Support**: Coming soon - Phone integration with AI call transcription
- **Social Messaging**: Coming soon - WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Instagram DM integration
- **Collision Detection**: Real-time indicators when multiple agents view the same conversation
- **Smart Assignment**: AI-powered routing based on skills, capacity, and conversation context

### AI-Powered Support
- **Autonomous AI Agents**: Customer-facing chatbots that resolve 60-70% of inquiries autonomously
- **Team Copilots**: On-demand AI assistance for agents with action execution capabilities (refunds, account updates, data lookups)
- **AI Writing Tools**: Tone adjustment, grammar correction, translation, and response suggestions
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Real-time detection of customer frustration and escalation triggers
- **Knowledge Management**: Vector-based semantic search across all help center content

### Workflow Automation
- **Visual Workflow Builder**: No-code drag-and-drop interface for complex automation
- **Smart Routing**: Intelligent assignment based on skills, language, timezone, and workload
- **SLA Management**: Automatic tracking with escalation triggers and breach notifications
- **Macros & Templates**: Reusable response templates with dynamic variable insertion
- **Scheduled Actions**: Time-based automation for follow-ups and status updates

### Quality Assurance (Pro Plan)
- **Auto-QA Scoring**: AI evaluates every conversation against custom rubrics
- **AI Feedback**: Personalized coaching suggestions for each agent
- **Custom Scorecards**: Build rubrics aligned to your quality standards
- **Supervisor Overrides**: Human review and adjustment of AI scores
- **Trend Analysis**: Identify coaching opportunities across your team

### Analytics & Reporting
- **Pre-built Dashboards**: Real-time metrics on resolution time, CSAT, and volume
- **Deflection Tracking**: Measure chatbot effectiveness and cost savings
- **ROI Calculator**: Track savings from AI automation vs legacy tools
- **Data Export**: CSV/Excel export for custom analysis
- **Custom Reports**: Filter by date range, agent, channel, or tag

### Help Centers (Self-Service)
- **Knowledge Base**: Create articles with rich text, images, and embedded videos
- **SEO Optimized**: Automatic sitemap generation and meta tag management
- **Multi-language**: Publish content in multiple languages with automatic detection
- **Search Analytics**: Track what customers search for to identify content gaps
- **1 Help Center (Standard)**: Single branded help center
- **Unlimited Help Centers (Pro)**: Separate help centers for different products/brands

## Security & Compliance

### Data Protection
- **Encryption**: AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
- **Data Residency**: Choose your data storage region (US, EU, APAC)
- **Backup & Recovery**: Automated daily backups with 30-day retention
- **GDPR Tools**: Data export, deletion, and consent management
- **SOC 2 Type II**: In progress (Q2 2025)

### Access Control
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Custom roles with granular permissions
- **Single Sign-On (SSO)**: Coming Q1 2025 - SAML 2.0, OAuth 2.0
- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Mandatory 2FA for admin roles
- **Audit Logs**: Complete activity tracking for compliance
- **IP Whitelisting**: Restrict access by IP address (Enterprise add-on)

# AI Suite - Complete AI Toolkit for Support Teams

## Overview

Pullse's AI Suite provides agent-facing AI tools including Copilot (on-demand AI assistant), AI writing tools, knowledge management, and conversation intelligence.

## Core Components

### Copilot - On-Demand AI Assistant
- **Action Execution**: AI performs tasks for agents (process refund, update account, lookup data) with one click
- **Response Suggestions**: Context-aware reply recommendations based on conversation history
- **Customer Insights**: Instant summaries of customer account, purchase history, previous conversations
- **Multi-tool Integration**: Pulls data from CRM, payment processor, shipping provider in one request
- **Natural Language Commands**: Agents ask Copilot in plain English ("refund their last order")

### AI Writing Tools
- **Tone Adjustment**: Rewrite responses to be more professional, friendly, empathetic, or concise
- **Grammar & Spelling**: Auto-correct errors with one click
- **Translation**: Real-time translation to/from 50+ languages
- **Expand/Shorten**: Adjust response length while maintaining meaning
- **Template Generation**: Create reusable macros from successful responses

### Knowledge Management
- **Semantic Search**: Find relevant help articles even with imprecise search terms
- **Auto-suggestions**: Recommended articles appear as agents type responses
- **Content Gaps**: Identify frequently asked questions without help articles
- **Version Control**: Track changes to knowledge base content over time
- **Multi-source**: Search across help centers, internal docs, Slack, and integrations

### Copilot Profiles (Unlimited on Pro)
- **Custom AI Personas**: Create specialized copilots for different teams or use cases
- **Skill-based**: Technical support copilot with dev docs, billing copilot with payment tools
- **Permission Scoping**: Limit what actions each copilot profile can execute
- **Knowledge Filtering**: Each profile searches only relevant content for its domain

## Key Features

### Context Awareness
- Pulls customer data from CRM automatically
- Shows conversation history across all channels
- Identifies customer sentiment and urgency level
- Suggests escalation when needed

### Action Execution
- **One-click Actions**: Execute common tasks without leaving the conversation
- **Approval Workflows**: Optional human confirmation for high-risk actions
- **Audit Logging**: Complete record of all AI-executed actions
- **Rollback Support**: Undo accidental actions when possible

### Learning & Improvement
- Learns from agent corrections and feedback
- Adapts to your team's most common responses
- Suggests new macros based on repetitive typing patterns
- Improves accuracy over time with usage

# Appo - Help Centers & Knowledge Base Platform

## Overview

Create self-service knowledge bases to deflect support inquiries, improve SEO, and empower customers to find answers 24/7. Branded help centers with rich content, search, and analytics.

## Key Features

### Content Creation
- **Rich Text Editor**: Format articles with headings, lists, images, videos, code blocks
- **Media Library**: Upload images, PDFs, videos for embedding in articles
- **Table of Contents**: Auto-generated navigation for long articles
- **Related Articles**: Link to relevant content for deeper exploration
- **Version History**: Track changes and revert to previous versions

### Organization
- **Categories & Collections**: Group articles by topic or product
- **Multi-level Navigation**: Nested categories for complex content hierarchies
- **Tags**: Add metadata for better search and filtering
- **Article Status**: Draft, published, archived with scheduling
- **Permissions**: Control which articles are public vs agent-only

### Customization
- **Branding**: Custom logo, colors, fonts to match your website
- **Domain Mapping**: Host help center on your domain (e.g., help.yourcompany.com)
- **Custom CSS**: Advanced styling for complete design control (Pro plan)
- **Navigation Menus**: Customize header/footer links and CTAs
- **Contact Forms**: Embed forms for users who can't find answers

### Search & Discovery
- **Semantic Search**: AI-powered search understands intent (not just keywords)
- **Autocomplete**: Suggest articles as users type
- **Popular Articles**: Highlight most-viewed content
- **Search Analytics**: Track what users search for to identify content gaps
- **Multilingual**: Publish articles in multiple languages with auto language detection

## SEO Optimization

- **Automatic Sitemap**: Generated for search engine indexing
- **Meta Tags**: Custom title, description, and Open Graph tags per article
- **Clean URLs**: SEO-friendly slugs (e.g., /help/how-to-reset-password)
- **Schema Markup**: Structured data for rich search results
- **Performance**: Fast loading times with CDN delivery

## Analytics & Insights

### Content Performance
- Page views per article
- Search-to-click conversion rate
- Average time on page
- Bounce rate
- Feedback (helpful/not helpful votes)

### Deflection Tracking
- % of searches that resulted in article views
- % of article viewers who then contacted support
- Estimated cost savings from self-service
- Most effective articles (highest deflection rate)

### Content Gaps
- Common searches with no results
- Articles with low helpfulness ratings
- Topics with high support volume but low help center coverage

## Integration with Support Platform

- **Chatbot Knowledge Source**: AI agents pull answers from help center articles
- **Agent Suggestions**: Recommend articles to customers during live chat
- **Macros**: Insert help center links in support replies
- **Unified Search**: Agents search help center while handling tickets

## Plan Limits

### Standard Plan
- **1 Help Center**: Single branded knowledge base
- All content creation features
- Unlimited articles and categories
- Basic analytics

### Pro Plan
- **Unlimited Help Centers**: Separate help centers for different products/brands
- All content creation features
- Unlimited articles and categories
- Advanced analytics + custom CSS
